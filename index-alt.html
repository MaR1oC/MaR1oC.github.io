About my GitHub & Bitbucket accounts

I regularly use GitHub/Bitbucket in order to share my code and inspirations. I firmly believe that we must be open! Although my thoughts may be complete to some extent, I do not hesitate to continuously push code so that others (as well as me) can be inspired and motivated to work on a project! ;)
